SPACE ORCS BRAWL

A local arena game where you fight with sentient spaceships.

← Back to Projects

Overview

Space Orcs Brawl is a local arena/party game where each player plays as one of 7 different spaceships, each with a different play style and set of abilities. You can play with up to 4 players in a game and there are multiple game modes such as soccer.

Contributions

For 'Space Orcs Brawl!', I was responsible for how each ship operated, what abilities they could use, and their overall ability identity. I focused on making sure each ship had its own combat style, such as Doc Croc, who is all about getting in close to deal a lot of damage quickly with multi-attack combos.

Other things I did on this project include:

  • Manage all ability and character data on the back end.
  • Discussing art direction with the artist to fit with gameplay.
  • Game balance of all characters, abilities etc.
  • Manage the Steam Page and Steamworks Admin.
