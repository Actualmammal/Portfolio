Overview

Reclimation Protocol in an in development project where you create RTS units, by constructing them with a factory game style construction line, connecting buildings like miners, smelters and crafters via conveyor belts.

Contributions

I was lead designer for this project, as such I designed all how all the mechanics and gameplay systems would work. Additionally I was also a gameplay programmer, assisting the lead programmer with individual mechanic implimentation and testing.
